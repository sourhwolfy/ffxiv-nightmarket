Freelance artist from Brazil, Game Dev graduate with a focus on 2D art and graphic design — specifically the study of color and how it shapes a character's presence. After years without proper equipment, I recently got back on my feet with a new tablet and PC, and I'm diving back in headfirst.

In FFXIV I've always gravitated toward Healers and Tanks — support roles have been my thing since I was a kid playing my first games. Been part of the XIV world since 2020, took a break, and now I'm back at it. My main class is Astrologian, though Reaper holds a special place as my favorite DPS. Outside of markets, I'm quietly working on a personal visual novel — first episode in the works.
